Safe shopping tips

Simple checks before buying.

  • Check seller rating and recent reviews, especially those with photos.
  • Compare shipping time and tracking availability.
  • Read product specs carefully such as sizes, voltage, and materials.
  • Prefer listings with clear return or refund policy.
  • Use secure payment methods and avoid off-platform payment requests.
