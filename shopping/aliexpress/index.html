Safe Shopping Tips

Before buying, check a few simple details to avoid mistakes and choose better listings.

  • Check seller rating and recent reviews, especially reviews with photos.
  • Compare shipping time, tracking availability, and estimated delivery dates.
  • Read product specs carefully, including size, voltage, material, and included items.
  • Prefer listings with clear return, refund, or buyer protection details.
  • Use secure payment methods and avoid off-platform payment requests.

Note: Product availability, prices, delivery estimates, and deals may change anytime. Always verify details on AliExpress before checkout.
