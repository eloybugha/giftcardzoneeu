Buying tips before checkout

  • Check connector type carefully such as USB-C, Lightning, or older cable versions.
  • Review recent buyer photos when buying cases, mounts, or charging items.
  • Read dimensions and model compatibility before purchasing accessories for specific phones.
  • Compare shipping options, tracking availability, and expected delivery windows.
  • Choose listings with clear refund terms and avoid unclear off-platform payment requests.
