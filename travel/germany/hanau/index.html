Hanau is a well-connected city in the German state of Hesse, located east of Frankfurt. It is associated with the Brothers Grimm and combines cultural landmarks, palace gardens, shopping areas and convenient transport connections.

Visitors can stay in the city center for restaurants and everyday convenience, near Philippsruhe Palace for quieter surroundings, or close to Hanau Central Station for regional and long-distance travel.

Hanau FAQ

Is Hanau worth visiting?

Yes. Hanau offers Philippsruhe Palace, parks, museums, cultural attractions and convenient access to Frankfurt and the surrounding region.

Where is the best place to stay in Hanau?

The city center is convenient for restaurants, shopping and transport. The Philippsruhe Palace area is suitable for visitors who prefer quieter and more scenic surroundings.

Is Hanau convenient for train travel?

Yes. Hanau Central Station offers useful regional and long-distance rail connections, including services toward Frankfurt, Fulda and Aschaffenburg.
