Aschaffenburg is a historic city in Lower Franconia, Bavaria, located beside the Main River. Its compact center combines palace architecture, museums, parks, restaurants and convenient transport links.

Visitors can stay near the Old Town for sightseeing and dining, close to Johannisburg Palace for major landmarks, or near the station for easy regional train connections.
