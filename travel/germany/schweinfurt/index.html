Schweinfurt is a historic city in Lower Franconia, Bavaria, located along the Main River. The city combines industrial heritage, museums, parks, riverside walks and a compact old center.

Visitors can choose accommodation near the Old Town for restaurants and sightseeing, near the river for relaxing views, or close to transport links for exploring Bavaria.
