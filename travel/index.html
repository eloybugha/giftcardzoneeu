Smart Hotel Booking Tips

Before choosing a hotel, compare prices, check location, and review cancellation terms.

1. Compare more than one platform

Hotel prices can vary between travel platforms. Checking Trip.com and Expedia can help you spot better value.

2. Choose the right area

Staying near transport, attractions, or business districts can save time and extra travel costs.

3. Check flexible booking

Look for cancellation rules, refund policies, taxes, and fees before completing your reservation.
