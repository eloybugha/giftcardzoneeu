Quick guide

Why Stay in Salzburg?

Salzburg is one of Austria’s most beautiful city-break destinations, known for music history, baroque architecture, mountain scenery, and easy access to nearby alpine towns.

Beautiful Old Town

Salzburg’s Altstadt is compact, walkable, and packed with historic streets, churches, cafés, viewpoints, and classic Austrian charm.

Perfect for music lovers

The city is strongly connected to Mozart, classical music, festivals, museums, and cultural experiences throughout the year.

Easy day trips

Salzburg works well as a base for Hallstatt, Berchtesgaden, Lake District routes, and scenic Austria or Bavaria travel plans.

Great train connections

The main station connects Salzburg with Vienna, Munich, Innsbruck, and other Central Europe destinations.
