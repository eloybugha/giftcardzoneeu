FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Brussels worth staying in?

Yes. Brussels is a strong choice for history, food, museums, business travel, and easy train connections around Belgium.

Where should first-time visitors stay in Brussels?

The city center or Grand Place area is usually the easiest choice because attractions, restaurants, shops, and transport are nearby.

Is the EU Quarter a good place to stay?

Yes, especially for business travelers or visitors who prefer modern hotels and quieter surroundings outside the busiest tourist streets.

Can you visit Bruges or Ghent from Brussels?

Yes. Brussels is a practical base for day trips to Bruges, Ghent, Antwerp, and other Belgium cities by train.
