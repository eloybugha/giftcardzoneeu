Mons Travel FAQ

Is Mons worth visiting?

Yes. Mons is worth visiting for its Grand Place, historic streets, museums, heritage sites, cafés, and relaxed Walloon city atmosphere.

Where should first-time visitors stay in Mons?

The Grand Place area is usually the best choice because it keeps restaurants, cafés, sightseeing spots, old streets, and city atmosphere close.

Is Mons good for a day trip from Brussels?

Yes. Mons can work as a day trip from Brussels by train, but staying overnight gives you more time to enjoy the city without rushing.

Is Mons better than Namur?

Mons feels more cultural and historic around its central square, while Namur is more riverside and scenic. Choose Mons for culture and heritage, or Namur for citadel views and river walks.
