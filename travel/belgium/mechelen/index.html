Mechelen Travel FAQ

Is Mechelen worth visiting?

Yes. Mechelen is worth visiting for its historic center, cathedral views, canals, cafés, museums, family attractions, and easy location between Brussels and Antwerp.

Where should first-time visitors stay in Mechelen?

The Grote Markt area is usually the best choice because it keeps cafés, restaurants, sightseeing spots, old streets, and city atmosphere close.

Is Mechelen good for a day trip from Brussels?

Yes. Mechelen can work as a day trip from Brussels by train, but staying overnight gives you more time to enjoy the city without rushing.

Is Mechelen better than Antwerp?

Mechelen is calmer, smaller, and easier for a relaxed stay, while Antwerp is bigger and better for shopping, nightlife, museums, and a stronger city atmosphere.
