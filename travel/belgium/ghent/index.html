Why visit Ghent?

A Historic Belgium City with Canals, Castles & Cozy Hotels

Ghent is one of Belgium’s most atmospheric city breaks, known for medieval architecture, canal views, old town streets, castle scenery, museums, restaurants, and relaxed evening walks.

  • Stay near the historic center for easy sightseeing
  • Choose canal-side areas for classic Ghent atmosphere
  • Enjoy castles, cafes, museums, restaurants, and old town walks
  • Use Ghent as a beautiful Belgium stop between Brussels and Bruges

Ghent Hotel Tips

For the easiest first stay, book near the city center or old town so you can walk to most attractions. For a more scenic trip, look for stays near the canals. Ghent is walkable and relaxed, so a well-located hotel can make sightseeing, dining, and evening walks much easier.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ghent worth staying in?

Yes. Ghent is one of Belgium’s best city breaks, especially for canals, old town streets, castle views, restaurants, and historic stays.

Where should first-time visitors stay in Ghent?

The city center or old town is usually the easiest choice because attractions, restaurants, canals, shops, and scenic streets are nearby.

Is Ghent good for couples?

Yes. Ghent is a strong romantic and relaxed destination because of its canals, historic buildings, cozy cafes, and peaceful walking areas.

Can you visit Ghent from Brussels?

Yes. Many travelers visit Ghent from Brussels by train, but staying overnight gives you more time to enjoy the city calmly.
