Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Hague worth staying in?

Yes. The Hague is great for travelers who want museums, elegant streets, international culture, and easy beach access.

Where should first-time visitors stay in The Hague?

The city center is usually the easiest choice because it keeps restaurants, museums, shopping, and attractions nearby.

Is Scheveningen a good place to stay?

Yes. Scheveningen is ideal if you want a coastal stay, beach walks, sea views, and a more relaxed hotel experience.

Can you visit Rotterdam from The Hague?

Yes. The Hague and Rotterdam are close enough for an easy train trip, making them good cities to combine in one itinerary.
