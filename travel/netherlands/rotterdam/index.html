Why Visit Rotterdam?

Rotterdam offers a completely different vibe from Amsterdam. Instead of canals and historic streets, Rotterdam focuses on futuristic architecture, skyline towers, waterfront districts, shopping, nightlife, and a modern European city atmosphere.
