Quick tips for Singapore stays
Watch the dates.

Prices jump during big events and holidays. Adjust your dates by 1–2 nights to see cheaper options.

Location vs. budget.

Marina Bay is iconic but pricey. For value, check areas like Bugis, Little India, or Chinatown with good MRT access.

Filter by reviews.

Use “8+ rating” or “Very good” filters to quickly find reliable properties.

Check cancellation rules.

Some promos are non-refundable. If your plans might change, choose flexible options when possible.
