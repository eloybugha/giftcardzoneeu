Why Use 12Go in the Philippines?

The Philippines is made up of thousands of islands, so transport planning can get tricky. 12Go is useful for travelers who want to compare schedules, check route options, and secure transport in advance.

Island-to-Island Travel

Ferries are a big part of Philippine travel. 12Go can make it easier to plan routes between islands without checking multiple operators one by one.

Bus & Van Booking

For long overland trips, buses and vans are still important. This is especially useful for routes outside major airport hubs.

Better Advance Planning

Booking ahead can help avoid sold-out schedules during holidays, long weekends, and peak tourism months.

Popular Philippines Routes to Check

These route examples reflect the kind of domestic transport travelers often search for in the Philippines, from island connections to major land journeys.

Manila to Bicol

A classic long-distance route for travelers heading south from Metro Manila toward Bicol and nearby ports. Good for those preparing for onward ferry connections.

Cebu to Bohol

One of the most popular island-hopping routes. This is ideal for travelers moving between two major Visayas destinations.

Batangas Ferry Connections

Batangas is an important jump-off point for ferry travel. Useful for domestic travelers heading to beach and island destinations.

Leyte to Surigao

A practical route for travelers moving between Visayas and Mindanao regions, especially when combining road and sea travel.

When 12Go Makes the Most Sense

Not every trip needs it, but for certain routes and travel styles, 12Go can be a very practical booking tool.

Use it for multi-stop trips: Helpful when your trip includes ferries, buses, vans, and transfers across several regions.
Use it during busy dates: Holiday travel in the Philippines can get crowded fast, so advance booking becomes more valuable.
Use it for route checking: Even if you are still comparing options, it helps to see schedules and plan your travel flow.
Use it for domestic connections: Especially useful where flights are not the only or best option for the journey.
