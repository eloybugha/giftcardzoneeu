Helpful information

Frequently asked questions

What can I book through MyTrip?

MyTrip allows travelers to search and book flights to domestic and international destinations. Available airlines, itineraries, prices and conditions depend on the selected route and dates.

Does GiftCardZoneEU process my booking?

No. GiftCardZoneEU provides destination information and affiliate links. Flight searches, payments, confirmations, changes and customer support are handled through MyTrip.

Are displayed flight prices guaranteed?

Flight availability and prices can change. Review the final itinerary, total price and fare conditions shown on MyTrip before completing your payment.

Should I check baggage and cancellation rules?

Yes. Always review baggage allowances, cancellation conditions, change fees, passenger details and airline requirements before confirming the booking.
