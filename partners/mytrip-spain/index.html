FAQ

Is it cheaper to book flight + hotel together?

Often yes — bundles can reduce total cost, especially for short trips and peak travel weeks. Always compare bundle vs. separate booking before checkout.

Which cities are best for first-time Spain travel?

Barcelona and Madrid are the most common starting points, with great transport links, day trips, and plenty of hotel options.

When should I book for better prices?

Prices change fast. If you see a good fare and your dates are fixed, booking earlier can help — especially for weekends and summer months.
