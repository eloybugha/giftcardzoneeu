How to use CheapTickets for Boston wisely

A simple Boston booking checklist before you click the final payment button.

Compare flexible dates

Boston prices can change depending on season, conferences, university calendars, sports events, holidays, route, airport choice, and availability. Try nearby dates if your schedule allows it.

📍

Check the location

For hotels, compare the price with the area. A cheaper stay far from your airport, meeting venue, university visit, event location, downtown area, or planned attractions may cost more in transport and time.

Before booking, check taxes, hotel fees, baggage rules, cancellation policy, parking fees, airport transfers, and the final price shown at checkout.
