Why visit the UK?

Historic cities, modern culture and varied landscapes

The United Kingdom offers famous landmarks, royal history, museums, theater, football, coastal scenery, countryside escapes and distinctive destinations across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.

  • Explore London landmarks, museums and entertainment districts.
  • Discover Edinburgh’s historic center and castle views.
  • Visit Manchester for football, music and urban culture.
  • Experience Birmingham, Liverpool, Glasgow and Belfast.
  • Combine city breaks with countryside or coastal journeys.

Popular UK destinations

Choose a city that matches your trip

Each UK destination offers a different travel experience, so compare airport access, hotel location and local transportation before booking.

London

Ideal for iconic landmarks, museums, shopping, theater, business travel and international flight connections.

Edinburgh

A historic Scottish capital known for its castle, Old Town, festivals and dramatic city views.

Manchester

Popular for football, music, shopping, nightlife, business events and regional transportation links.

Birmingham

A central English city with shopping, conferences, dining, cultural attractions and strong rail connections.

Glasgow

Known for architecture, live music, museums, restaurants and access to western Scotland.

Liverpool

A waterfront city associated with music history, football, museums and maritime heritage.

Belfast

Northern Ireland’s capital offers historic landmarks, waterfront attractions and access to scenic day trips.

Cardiff

The Welsh capital combines a compact center, castle, sporting events and convenient regional travel.

Oxford

A historic university city suited to cultural visits, architecture, museums and short breaks from London.

Useful UK travel details

Things to consider before booking

London airports

London is served by several airports, including Heathrow, Gatwick, Stansted, Luton and London City. Check the exact airport before booking.

Domestic connections

Flights may connect London with Edinburgh, Glasgow, Belfast, Manchester and other UK destinations, depending on availability.

Rail alternatives

Some city-to-city journeys may also be practical by train. Compare total journey time, baggage needs and accommodation location.

Entry requirements

Travel-document and entry requirements depend on nationality and may change. Verify current official requirements before departure.

Seasonal demand

Prices and hotel availability may change around school holidays, festivals, sporting events and the summer travel season.

Booking support
