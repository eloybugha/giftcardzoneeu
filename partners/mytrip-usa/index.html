Before Booking Your Flight

  • Enter every passenger name exactly as shown on the passport.
  • Review baggage allowances for each flight segment.
  • Check whether the fare includes changes or cancellations.
  • Confirm airport terminals and connection times.
  • Save your booking confirmation and payment receipt.

USA Travel Planning Tips

  • Compare direct flights with connecting routes before paying.
  • Check local transport from the arrival airport.
  • Review current passport and entry requirements separately.
  • Consider travel insurance for expensive or multi-city trips.
  • Allow enough time for immigration and baggage collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I compare flights to different USA cities with MyTrip?

Yes. You can search available flights to destinations such as New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Las Vegas and Chicago. Results depend on your departure airport, dates and current airline availability.

Which airports should I compare when flying to New York?

New York travelers can compare John F. Kennedy International Airport, Newark Liberty International Airport and LaGuardia Airport. Consider the total fare and transfer time to your final destination.

When should I book flights to the United States?

Prices vary according to demand, season and route availability. When your dates are fixed, comparing options early may be useful, particularly before holidays and busy travel periods.

Are baggage fees included in every MyTrip fare?

Not necessarily. Baggage allowances depend on the selected airline, route and fare type. Review the baggage information shown during booking before completing payment.
