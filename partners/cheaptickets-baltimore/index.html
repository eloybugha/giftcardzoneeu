Why visit Baltimore?

A waterfront city with history, culture and major events

Baltimore combines harbor attractions, historic neighborhoods, museums, seafood restaurants and convenient access to other destinations across Maryland and the Washington–Baltimore region.

  • Explore the Inner Harbor and surrounding waterfront attractions.
  • Walk through historic Fell’s Point and its cobblestone streets.
  • Visit museums, markets and cultural landmarks across the city.
  • Plan trips around Orioles baseball or Ravens football games.
  • Stay near the cruise terminal, convention venues or BWI Airport.

Where to stay

Popular Baltimore areas for different trips

The most convenient neighborhood depends on whether your priority is waterfront sightseeing, nightlife, sporting events, business meetings or airport access.

Inner Harbor

A practical central base for major visitor attractions, waterfront walks, museums and family-oriented Baltimore trips.

Harbor East

A modern waterfront district with hotels, restaurants and convenient access to both the Inner Harbor and Fell’s Point.

Fell’s Point

A historic waterfront neighborhood known for cobblestone streets, restaurants, pubs and a lively evening atmosphere.

Federal Hill

A popular district near the harbor with local dining, neighborhood character and access to central attractions.

Downtown Baltimore

Useful for business travel, conventions, cultural venues and access to stadiums and central transportation connections.

BWI Airport area

Convenient for early departures, late arrivals, airport stopovers and travelers prioritizing road or rail connections.

Useful details before booking

BWI Airport

Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport is the main airport serving Baltimore. Compare airport transportation and hotel location before choosing a property.

Events and sports

Hotel demand may rise during major conventions, festivals, Orioles games and Ravens games. Compare availability early when visiting for a scheduled event.

Cruise travel

Travelers departing from Baltimore’s cruise terminal may consider a pre-cruise hotel, transportation plan and enough arrival time before embarkation.

Getting around

CheapTickets Baltimore booking FAQ

Can I compare Baltimore flights and hotels with CheapTickets?

Yes. CheapTickets lets travelers search flights, accommodation, vacation packages and car rentals for Baltimore. Availability and prices depend on your selected dates and booking conditions.

Which Baltimore neighborhood is best for first-time visitors?

The Inner Harbor is a convenient starting point for many first-time visitors because it provides access to several waterfront attractions. Harbor East, Fell’s Point and Federal Hill are also popular choices.

Which airport should I select for Baltimore?

Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport uses the code BWI and is the principal airport serving Baltimore. Always verify the airport shown in your itinerary before paying.

Can a flight and hotel package be cheaper?

A package may provide competitive value for some dates, but not every combination is cheaper. Compare the package total against the separate flight and hotel prices, including all applicable fees.

When should I book a Baltimore hotel?

Booking earlier may provide more choices during major events, conventions, sports weekends and cruise departure periods. Prices and availability can still change at any time.

Who handles my reservation after booking?

CheapTickets or the travel provider shown in your confirmation handles payments, booking changes, cancellations and customer service. GiftCardZoneEU does not access or manage reservations.
