Common questions

MyTrip Turkey FAQ

Can I compare both flights and hotels for Turkey with MyTrip?

MyTrip allows travelers to search available flight options, while hotel or package availability may depend on the selected route, dates and booking offer.

Which Turkish cities are popular for first-time visitors?

Istanbul is a common starting point. Travelers also frequently consider Cappadocia for its landscapes, Antalya for beaches and Izmir for access to Turkey's Aegean coast.

Should I compare Istanbul Airport and Sabiha Gökçen Airport?

Yes. Comparing flights through Istanbul Airport (IST) and Sabiha Gökçen Airport (SAW) may reveal different fares, schedules and ground-transfer times.

When should I book flights to Turkey?

Airfares vary according to season, route, availability and demand. Travelers with fixed dates may benefit from comparing options earlier, especially around holidays and peak summer periods.
