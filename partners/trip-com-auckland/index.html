Trip.com Auckland hotel questions

What is the best area to stay in Auckland?

Auckland CBD is usually the most practical option for first-time visitors because it provides access to major attractions, restaurants, shopping and transport. Viaduct Harbour is suitable for waterfront stays.

Is Auckland CBD a good place to stay?

Yes. Auckland CBD works well for first-time visitors, business trips, short city breaks, shopping, restaurants and public transport.
