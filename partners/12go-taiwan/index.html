Taiwan × 12Go – quick questions
Is GiftCardZoneEU the one running the transport?
No. We’re an affiliate partner that helps you discover routes and then sends you to 12Go to complete your booking. 12Go works with official operators across Taiwan.
Do I pay extra by booking through this page?
No. Ticket prices are the same as booking directly on 12Go. In some cases there may even be promos or discounts visible in their search.
Where will I get my ticket?
Your confirmation and ticket details come from 12Go by email after payment. Some routes use e-tickets, while others may require ticket pick-up — 12Go will show the exact instructions.
Who do I contact if I have issues with my booking?
If you have questions about changes, cancellations or delays, please contact 12Go directly using the support details in your booking confirmation email.
