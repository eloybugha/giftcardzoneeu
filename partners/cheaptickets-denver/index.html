Best areas to stay in Denver

Downtown Denver is convenient for events, dining, business trips, and city attractions. LoDo and Union Station work well for nightlife, restaurants, train access, and walkable city stays.

Cherry Creek is popular for shopping, polished hotels, and a calmer upscale stay. Airport-area hotels can be practical for early flights, layovers, and road trips where convenience matters more than sightseeing.
