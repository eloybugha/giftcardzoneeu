Smart travel tips

These checks can help prevent avoidable mistakes and unexpected costs.

Match passport details

Enter names exactly as shown on the passport and double-check dates of birth before payment.

🧳

Check baggage rules

Low fares may include only a small cabin bag, so compare the total after adding checked luggage.

📍

Review hotel location

Check the hotel on a map and estimate travel time to airports, stations, beaches or attractions.

📅

Compare flexible rates

Refundable bookings may provide better protection when your dates or plans are not fully confirmed.

Frequently asked questions

MyTrip Thailand booking questions

Can I compare both flights and hotels for Thailand on MyTrip?

Yes. You can compare flight and hotel options for popular destinations such as Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai, Krabi and Pattaya.

Which Thailand destinations are best for first-time visitors?

Bangkok is the most common starting point. Add Chiang Mai for culture and food, or Phuket and Krabi for beaches and island trips.

Should I compare bundles with separate bookings?

Yes. Compare the total flight-and-hotel package against booking separately, including baggage, taxes, transfers and cancellation conditions.

When should I book Thailand flights and hotels?

Booking earlier can help when your dates are fixed, especially during holidays and peak-season weeks. Prices and availability may still change quickly.

Which Bangkok airport should I choose?

Bangkok Suvarnabhumi Airport is coded BKK, while Don Mueang International Airport is coded DMK. Compare both airports, but also check transfer time and transport costs.

What should I verify before paying?

Check names, dates, airport codes, hotel location, baggage, cancellation rules, included services and the final total before completing payment.
